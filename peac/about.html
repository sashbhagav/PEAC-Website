PEAC

Mission

PEAC focuses on serving students in under-privileged areas to help them pass their standardized testing exams as well as prepare them for college. We also aim to help students understand the importance of education through various workshops and bringing in guest speakers.

PEAC's mission is led by campus chapters that go out into their community to serve the schools, children and fellow citizens. The beauty of this organization is that each campus chapter is built differently to address the communities' needs. Want to tutor in your surrounding schools? Do you want to hold exciting career exploration days for students? Want to offer college readiness workshops? Want to hold seminars on the importance of healthy eating? PEAC progressive agenda is to address the issues of today which revolve around education.

Steps to create a Chapter

1

Assemble an officer team

Create a minimum team of 3 people and a possible sponsor to kickstart your chapter.

  • President
  • Vice-President
  • Secretary & Treasurer
2

Contact possible locations

Contact possible schools in which you can provide the services your chapter will offer.

  • Elementary Schools
  • Middle Schools
  • High Schools
  • College or University
3

Recruiting

Once you have a well established chapter, start recruiting more people to your officer team. You can also recruit people to help with your services.

  • Hold meetings
  • Orientations
  • Start Tutoring!
